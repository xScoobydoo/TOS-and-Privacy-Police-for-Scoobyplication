Scoobyplication — Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for Scoobyplication. This document explains how we handle and protect any information you may provide when using our Discord bot.

Scoobyplication does not collect any personal information beyond what is provided by the Discord API. We may store limited user data (such as user IDs, guild IDs, and command usage statistics) strictly for functionality and analytics purposes.

Any data collected is used exclusively to improve the bot’s performance, maintain its features, and ensure compliance with Discord’s Terms of Service.

4. Data Storage & Security

All stored information is kept securely and is not shared with third parties. We implement reasonable security measures to protect data from unauthorized access.

5. Data Removal

Users can request deletion of any stored data by contacting us at xscoobydoo.dev@gmail.com. We will process all valid deletion requests as quickly as possible.

6. Third-Party Services

Scoobyplication does not share or sell any user data to third-party services. Any integrations (such as Discord) are governed by their own privacy policies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the bot after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out at xscoobydoo.dev@gmail.com.
